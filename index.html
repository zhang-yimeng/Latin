Origin

Cuba is the birthplace of Latin dance and Latin music. At first, Latin music and dance was a way of expressing people's victory or harvest, and it gradually developed into a way for young people to express their love. In the course of its development, Latin dance was rejected because the action was too enthusiastic, the expression of emotion was too straightforward and without any constraints, but this did not affect the development of Latin dance. The irresistible charm finally made Latin dance popular in the world.
